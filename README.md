# FarmCart 🚜🛒  

## Empowering Farmers with a Digital Marketplace  

### Overview  

FarmCart is a **smart agricultural e-commerce platform** designed to help farmers **buy and sell agricultural products**, including seeds, fertilizers, tools, and fresh produce. The platform enhances **accessibility, transparency, and efficiency** in the agricultural supply chain.  

## Key Features  

✅ **Farmer & Buyer Authentication** – Secure login and registration for farmers and buyers.  
✅ **Product Listings & Categories** – Farmers can list products with images, prices, and descriptions.  
✅ **Search & Filters** – Easy product discovery with search and category-based filtering.  
✅ **Order Management** – Buyers can place orders, and farmers can track sales.  
✅ **Payment Integration** – Secure online transactions for hassle-free purchases.  
✅ **Real-Time Notifications** – Updates on orders, payments, and product inquiries.  
✅ **Multi-Language Support** – Ensuring accessibility for diverse users.  

## Technology Stack  

- **Frontend:** React.js  
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express, MongoDB  
- **Notifications:** Firebase Cloud Messaging (Real-time alerts)
